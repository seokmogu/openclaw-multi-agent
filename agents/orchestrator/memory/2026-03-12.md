### Key Architecture
- **Backend:** Fastify v5 + TypeScript, port 3001
- **Frontend:** Next.js 15 App Router PWA, port 3000
- **DB:** PostgreSQL + PostGIS (docker-compose: `postgis/postgis:16-3.4`)
- **Matching engine:** PostGIS `ST_DWithin` 5km radius, composite score (distance 40% + rating 25% + category 20% + activity 15%)
- **Real-time:** Socket.io 4.8.x — 15s offer timeout, accept/reject
- **Auth:** JWT (1h) + refresh (30d) in localStorage
- **Payment:** Toss Payments stub (no real PG)
- **Rate limiting:** 100 req/min global, 10 req/min for /auth/*
- **Admin:** X-Admin-Token protected /admin/* routes
